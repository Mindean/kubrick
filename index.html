Stanley Kubrick

The man who made cinema something else

Stanley Kubrick next to a vintage film camera.

Here's a time line of Stanley Kubrick's life:

  • 1928 - Born in Manhattan, New York, United States.
  • 1951 - Day of the Fight / Flying Padre
  • 1953 - Fear and Desire / The Seafarers
  • 1955 - Killer's Kiss
  • 1956 - The Killing
  • 1957 - Paths of Glory
  • 1960 - Spartacus
  • 1962 - Lolita
  • 1964 - Dr. Strangelove
  • 1968 - 2001: A Space Odyssey
  • 1971 - A Clockwork Orange
  • 1975 - Barry Lyndon
  • 1980 - The Shining
  • 1987 - Full Metal Jacket
  • 1999 - Eyes Wide Shut
  • 1999 - Dies at the age of 70.
